text(St)ream

Imagine you could stream your favorite song from anywhere, now you can!

All you need to do in order to take advantage of text(St)ream is send a simple text message and we would provide you with names of songs, lyrics for your favorite songs, and most importantly audio stream straight to your phone in the form of a phone call.

Genius-As-A-Service

Using our own developed Genius-As-A-Service we are able to pull the lyrics for the latest hip-hop and rock songs, you know... just to keep you rockin'! (or trappin' -- whatever is your favorite).

twilio

tex(St)ream uses twilio's amazing voice and sms service for sending and receiving messages. And most importantly getting the music into your ears!

SoundCloud API

We retrieve the different requested songs through SoundCloud's API supporting SoundCloud's billions of music uploads.

wit.ai

By using wit.ai's robust natural language processing system, we present the ease of use of querying for lyrics, music info, and yo' songs!
